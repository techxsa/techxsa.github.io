TechxSA Solution is an oil and services company established to source and implement the most advanced technologies in the oil and gas industry across various regions. With extensive industry knowledge and strong networks with both existing and potential clients, we are well-equipped to offer a wide range of products and services locally and internationally.

TechxSA Solution has a dedicated team of experts capable of handling the entire spectrum of geophysical and geological consulting services, including but not limited to:

Seismic Acquisition Design & Quality Control (QC)

Design seismic surveys and ensure they are conducted effectively to capture high-quality data. Monitor data acquisition processes and implement quality control measures to maintain data integrity.

Seismic Data Processing & QC

Process raw seismic data to enhance signal quality and remove noise. Ensure processed data meets required standards through rigorous quality control checks.


Seismic & Non-Seismic Data Interpretation Analysis

Interpret seismic and non-seismic geophysical data to identify and analyze subsurface geological features. Perform attribute analysis to extract additional information about the subsurface data.

Integrated Geophysical & Geological Data Analysis

Combine and analyze geophysical and geological data to develop a comprehensive understanding of subsurface conditions. Integrate various datasets to create detailed models and interpretations.

Development Variable Velocity Modelling

Develop and refine velocity models to improve the accuracy of seismic imaging. Use these models to correct for variations in subsurface velocities.


Structure and Reservoir Modelling

Develop and refine structural and reservoir models to accurately represent subsurface geology. Use these models for exploration and production planning.
